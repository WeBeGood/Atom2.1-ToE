\section{Core Equations}
Maxwell in differential forms:
\begin{align}
 dF &= 0,\\
 d\star F &= J.
\end{align}
In vacuum, $J=0$.

\section{Topology Hook}
If $dF=0$, then locally $F=dA$ for a 1-form potential $A$. Global existence depends on topology (cohomology).
