\section{Circular Basis}
Define circular basis vectors:
\begin{equation}
\hat{e}_{\pm} = \frac{\hat{x} \pm i\hat{y}}{\sqrt{2}}.
\end{equation}
These correspond to definite rotation sense of the real electric field under stated conventions.
